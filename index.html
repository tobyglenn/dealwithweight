Log your weight everyday

  • Do this using a custom spreadsheet that tells you if your within your wieght range (You may gain weight but still be in your 10 day range and actually losing weight overall)
  • One key is what you track is what you succeed at and building habits takes time, be patient

Things to do (four pillars of health):

Food

  • eat whole foods first
  • track what you eat to
  • try different approaches to see what works for you, ask yourself how do I feel the next day after eating something.

Exercise

  • pick things that are fun for you
  • if your really big (as I was) start with walking, get outside and move more
  • focus on how you feel during the workout, is it fun, is it sustainable

Sleep

  • Track to make sure you get a good amount of sleep
  • see how you feel first thing when you wake up

Stress

  • Box breathing
  • Activities and outdoor time
  • Meditation

Who its worked for

After going through treatments and getting better he ran and farmed to lose the weight while using the following tracking system to measure his weight and guarantee his weight loss goals.
